Katherine Houston

Three design directions for katherinehoustonart.com — choose your favorite to develop further

Concept 01
Gallery Standard

White-cube gallery aesthetic inspired by Gagosian and Pace Gallery. Split-screen hero with studio portrait left, text right. Gallery representation bar for immediate legitimacy signal. Work grid with hover-reveal info. Most institutional and prestigious feel.

Inspired by: Gagosian, White Cube, Pace Gallery

Concept 02
Immersive Dark

Dark, dramatic, contemporary. Full-width hero with floating artwork panels showing the vibrant abstracts against near-black — they glow. Stats bar, gold accents, "Acquire" language. Process section explains the Plexiglas technique in 3 steps. Collector-oriented.

Inspired by: Anish Kapoor, Olafur Eliasson, contemporary gallery openings

Concept 03
The Artisan's Story

Warm, editorial, story-driven. Cream tones, personal journey from finance to art, timeline, Instagram feed grid, individual gallery cards. Full process explainer (the Plexiglas technique is a huge differentiator — lean into it). Most conversion-friendly for social traffic.

Inspired by: editorial art blogs, Squarespace fine art portfolios, personal branding
